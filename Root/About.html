About me



Hello and welcome to my website!
My name is Samantha Dejah Kitchener, and I am an artist who is passionate about creating and expressing myself through various mediums. Since a young age, I have been drawn to art and its ability to evoke emotions, tell stories, and create connections between people.

Over the years, I have explored various art forms, including drawing, painting, makeup artistry, and digital design. Each of these mediums has challenged me in unique ways and has allowed me to develop my creative vision.

As I delved deeper into the world of makeup artistry, I discovered a passion for transforming faces and enhancing natural beauty. This led me to explore the world of digital design and user experience, where I found new ways to bring my artistic vision to life.

Today, I work as a freelance artist and designer, helping clients bring their visions to life. With a background in traditional art and makeup artistry, I bring a unique perspective to my work in digital design and user experience. I believe that art is not just about creating something beautiful, but also about connecting with people and creating meaningful experiences.

My ultimate goal as an artist and designer is to use my skills to make a positive impact on the world. Whether it's creating designs that make people's lives easier or using my art to raise awareness about important issues, I want my work to contribute to a better world.

Thank you for visiting my website, and I hope you enjoy exploring my portfolio. If you're interested in working with me, please don't hesitate to get in touch!
